How to Play

Collect 💧 water drops to earn points and help your community!

  • Desktop: Use ← / → to move, Space to jump
  • Mobile: Use on-screen arrows + Jump button
  • Each 💧 = +10 pts
  • Hitting 🪨 or 🗑️ = −10 pts and counts as 1 hit
  • 3 hits = Game Over
  • Unlock emoji heroes at Levels 3, 6, 9...
